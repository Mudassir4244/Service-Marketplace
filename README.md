# Service-Marketplace

Service Marketplace is a modern online platform designed to help non-technical workers such as plumbers, carpenters, electricians, mechanics, painters, and other skilled service providers connect with customers easily. The platform allows users to search for trusted professionals, book services, and communicate directly in a simple and user-friendly environment. Service providers can create their profiles, showcase their skills, manage bookings, and grow their business online.

The platform includes powerful features such as a **review and rating system** that helps customers choose reliable workers based on previous feedback and experiences. It also offers a **real-time chat system** that allows instant communication between customers and service providers for discussing job details, pricing, and availability. Users can enjoy a personalized experience with **Dark Mode and Light Mode themes**, making the platform comfortable to use at any time of day.

To serve a wider audience, Service Marketplace supports **English and Urdu localization**, allowing users to use the platform in their preferred language. The system is built with a responsive design, making it fully accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Overall, Service Marketplace aims to digitize local services, create more job opportunities for skilled workers, and make finding trusted services faster, easier, and more convenient for everyone.
